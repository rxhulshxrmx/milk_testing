About This MVP

This is a minimum viable product demonstrating the concept of rapid milk quality testing using colorimetric indicators and image analysis.

In a full implementation, the system would:

  • Use machine learning to accurately classify milk samples
  • Store results in a database for tracking
  • Generate detailed PDF reports
  • Provide trends and analytics over time
